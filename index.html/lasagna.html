Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

This lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It’s the perfect comfort food for family dinners

Easy to prepare and loved by everyone, this recipe will quickly become a household favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Sauté beef, onion, and garlic until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
